How Confirmly works

Confirmly helps you stop second guessing whether you already did something.

  • Tap to confirm standard items.
  • Important items require a press and hold.
  • Notes can be saved with confirmations.
  • Archive hides items you no longer need.
  • Archived items can be restored later.
  • All item history is stored locally in your browser on this device.
